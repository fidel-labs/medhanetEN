About Us.

MedhanET is a medicine search engine which is first of its kind in Ethiopia with broad reach and vision. It was developed in the year 2020 from the realization that majority of Ethiopians are inconvenienced in finding the prescription medicines that could potentially save their lives. This is due to the lack of centralised system that helps them locate and buy these medicines. This coupled with the lack of rigorous medication review regimes individuals were failing to follow up after being prescribed medication by their physicians. We found a way to streamline the whole process in order to make it easier for everyone to heal.

Through our interventions and locally-built technology, we’re doing more than just finding those medicines. We care about your well-being, and aim to do everything in our power in order to make sure you’re receiving the treatment you need and deserve.

​With our professional staff of pharmacists and doctors and a personal touch, we’re accomplishing the mission we set out to achieve: we’re helping the sick get better.

What We Do.

MedhanET is a medicine search engine. We make our customers lives easier by finding the medicines they're looking for through our network of pharmacies and provide them the directions and contact points for these pharmacies. Through our streamlined approach we aim to improve patient's medicine management in order for them to focus on getting better
