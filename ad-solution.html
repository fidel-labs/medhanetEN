MARKETING SOLUTIONS FOR HEALTHCARE BRANDS.

We've just released our marketing solution for healthcare businesses who are interested in reaching potential customers searching for their medicines in Ethiopia.

Introducing MedhanET Ad Solutions

What is MedhanET Ad Solutions?
MedhanET Ad Solutions is a brand new online marketing solution which makes targeting audiences in the quest for improving their own or their loved ones health using our drug search engine. Healthcare brands can now reach audiences on MedhanET websites (in Amharic and English) users who have been searching for different medicines targeting their messages based on Pharmacology and Drug Name.

How does it work?
Advertisers will now be able to run targeted banner ads on our websites (https://am.medhan.et and https.en.medhan.et) on prominent positions on the pages where users are searching for drugs and medical equipment. For example; users who are searching for a cardiovascular medicines can be targeted by a cardiovascular speciality clinic or hospital. 

How do Advertisers benefit from this?
Advertisers can run direct response campaigns by driving traffic to their landing pages or websites based on the user's search behaviour on our search engine. Advertisers will not have contact details of the customers (as we don't have it either) but will be able to reach a targeted niche audience qualified further by the nature of the searches they've made on the search engine.

How much does it cost?

- Advertiser Name
- Product or Service to be promoted
- Advertiser Website or Social Channel URL
- Goal: Direct Response or Build Awareness
- Budget available
- Tentative Campaign Date
