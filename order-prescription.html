Order Prescription Medicines

 Why do we exist?

The difficulty in finding medicines easily in Ethiopia is a common knowledge and a challenge that many face. On top of the illness we have to worry and manage finidng these medicines is a big hassle that has costed many lives - simply because they couldn't find the medcines that could have saved their lives nearby.

MedhanET aims to solve all that and streamline the process online

 Are we a pharmacy?

We are NOT a pharmacy. Nor do we sell any medicines ourselves. But we developed a technology which we believe will help patients with a prescription to search, purchase and deliver the medicines.

​We don't process orders without a Prescription currently. 

 How much does it cost?

We'll pass on the cost of the medicines to you as you'd normally pay at a pharmacy. In addition we'll charge a minimal Service charge between ETB 70 - 90 per delivery

 How do you order Prescription Medicines with MedhanET?

If you have a prescription please first search for the name of the medicine by clicking on the 'Find Medicines Near You' button below. Also, please enter your contact details (Name, Phone, etc...) in the spaces provided 

FIND MEDICINES NEAR YOU

 What happens next?

We'll start searching for your medciines through our network of pharmacies, drug stores and other licensed shops and will aim to respond with 24 Hours on whether we found your medicine or not.

If found, we'll call you too confirm the medicine, total cost, delivery location and take payment if you prefer to use Card Payment, Bank Transfer or Mobile Banking. We also accept Cash on Delivery.
