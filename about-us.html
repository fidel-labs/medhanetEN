About Us

MedhanET was born from a realization that majority of Ethiopians are inconvenienced in finding the prescription medicines that could potentially save their lives. This is due to the lack of centralised system that helps them locate and buy these medicines. This coupled with the lack of rigorous medication review regimes individuals were failing to follow up after being prescribed medication by their physicians. We found a way to streamline the whole process in order to make it easier for everyone to heal.

Through our interventions and locally-built technology, we’re doing more than just finding those medicines. We care about your well-being, and aim to do everything in our power in order to make sure you’re receiving the treatment you need and deserve.

​With our professional staff of pharmacists and doctors and a personal touch, we’re accomplishing the mission we set out to achieve: we’re helping the sick get better.
