Prescription Reminder Service

 What is a Prescription Reminder?

Our lives are typically busy with work, study or other areas. And for those of us that take repeat prescription medicines, we may forget to take our medicines as prescribed. Or we might run out of our medicines and need a re-fill. Our Prescription Reminder service is designed to send you our customers alert on taking your meds daily, weekly or monthly. Also, for MedhanET Customers we will send SMS Alerts 3 - 7 Days prior to their current supply finishes.  Based on the customer's wishes we'll locate the medicines and deliver them wherever they want.

 Can I customise the reminders?

Absolutely. You can customise the format of the reminders (Calendar Alert, SMS or Email), the receipient of alert (Patient or Carer), Frequency of alert (Daily, Weekly or Monthly)

 How much does it cost?

The cost of the Prescription Reminder Service is totally FREE for MedhanET customers
